Vision Analysis and Security (VAS) Lab

is led by Dr. Yuezun Li and is part of the OUC AI Research Center. The lab focuses on multimedia forensics and computer vision, and has achieved a series of research outcomes published in top-tier conferences and journals, including NeurIPS, ICCV, CVPR, ECCV, TIP, TIFS, TDSC, etc.

Research areas:
Multimedia forensics: face forensics; image/video manipulation detection...
Computer vision: general/underwater detection/tracking/generation...
