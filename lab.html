Welcome to VASLab

Visual Analysis & Security (VAS) Lab is part of the Institue of Artificial Intelligence at OUC, led by Dr. Yuezun Li. The VAS lab conducts research on computer vision and machine learning to analyze, authenticate, and safeguard visual data against manipulation and misuse. There are three main areas of research:

  • Multimedia forensics. Developing digital forensics algorithms for images, videos, and audio, including passive and active defense, across both general and face-centric scenarios.
  • Vision algorithms. Studying fundamental computer vision tasks such as classification, detection, tracking, and generation, in both general and underwater environments.
  • Vision security. Investigating security challenges related to the training and deployment of vision models.

Our lab has achieved a series of research outcomes published in top-tier conferences and journals, including NeurIPS, ICCV, CVPR, ECCV, TIP, TIFS, TDSC, etc. We welcome students passionate about research and emerging technologies, with a love for mathematics and programming, to join our lab.
